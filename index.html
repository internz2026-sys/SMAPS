What makes it different

Not another off-the-shelf school system

Most systems make your school fit their template. SMAPS is built the other way around.

Configured to your school, not a template

Grade levels, sections, subjects, grading weights, fee structures, report-card layouts, and staff roles are set to how your school actually runs — including the DepEd MATATAG curriculum and Senior High strands.

AI that knows how your school works

A built-in assistant answers staff how-to questions, grounded in SMAPS's own knowledge base rather than generic guesses — and AI helps flag duplicate students and match siblings during enrollment.

Built for Philippine schools

DepEd-aligned electronic class records and SF9-style report cards, GCash and Maya payments, and peso ledgers — not a foreign system bent to fit.
